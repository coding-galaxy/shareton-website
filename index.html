What We Do

We are proudly a non-profit organization. Many people like you help us stand up to reach and help those in need. We rely on your donations to carry out our mission. Shareton knows that our strength lies not only in the words we stand by, but most importantly through the actions of our initiatives.

Our Goals

Our main goal in Shareton, is to promote great opportunities for those in need. With access to the right resources. Giving back to our community, In Shareton you can donate food, clothes, shelter, and time by delivering these donations.

We strive to find new strategies for dealing with challenges. Donations Drop-Off is something that we take very seriously, and our team is working each and every day to make a positive impact.

Shareton facilitates the donations process by connecting people to each other more efficiently. We allow the donor to donate with just a few clicks, and offer a convent layout for the donations for the collector.
